Soufle

Delicious low calorie, low sugar desert for your pleasure, more liquidity makes more tasteful.

Ingredients

  1. 2 Egg
  2. 100 gram sugar
  3. 200 ml milk
  4. 1 pack of chocalates
  5. 50 ml oil
  6. 200 gram whole flour

Steps

  • Put all ingredients in a mixing bowl.
  • Mix them until they became liquid.
  • Use soufle plates to divide them and put a chocalte bar every one.
  • Cook them 180 degree 12 minutes then they are ready!
