Rice and Chicken

It's a classic meal also known as a bodybuilder meal. I can eat this meal everyday!

Ingredients

  1. Chicken breast (300 g)
  2. 180 gram rice (One glass)
  3. Olive oil or butter (40g)
  4. Salt, cajun and black pepper

Steps

  • Boil the rice with 2x water then its size.
  • Chop the chicken like small squares.
  • Fry the chickens with spice.
  • A little bit wait and your meal is ready!
