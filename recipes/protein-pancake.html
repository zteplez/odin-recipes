Protein Pancake

Delicious pancake with a lot of protein and healty carbs for getting fit.

Ingredients

  • 3 Medium Eggs
  • 1 Banana
  • 50 grams of oat
  • 1 tablespoon Cacao

Steps

  1. Get a mixing bowl and put all ingredients inside.
  2. Use mixer to acquire liquid form of ingredients.
  3. Heat frying pan and use spray fat.
  4. Empty it mixing bowl into frying pan and keep it low heat.
  5. Once you see the solid top of the pancake reverse the face and cook 2-3 minutes.
  6. Bon appetit
